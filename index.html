About

The Medlit Society is a Vision originating from Eight Medical Students(of 2018 batch, King George's Medical University, Lucknow, India) to fill the Void of Lack of an organised Students-only Branch for ExtraCurricular Activities where alongwith their Medical Career 'Medicos' round the Globe could showcase their Talents and be recognised for their Talents and Hobbies😊.
